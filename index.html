Comfortable Self Catering Accommodation In Garies

At De Kleine Werf we offer our guests a variety of prime accommodation options to suit every requirement

Our Units are decorated in a warm an personal style, hankering back to bygone days, but without the sacrifice of modern comfort.

Garies is much more than a purely convenient overnight stop for those travelling between the Cape and Namibia.

Located plumb in the centre of the big heart of Namaqualand, this small country town is the ideal base from which to explore the floral splendor which carpets the area annually after good and timeous rainfall.

Even during dry years when our fabled wildflowers remain dormant, our vast and ancient landscapes invite the traveler to experience our treasure trove of rare fauna and flora.

Our friendly and hospitable community thrives in a malaria free zone, which is reassuring to our visitors.

So when looking for accommodation in Garies, give us a try.
